THE PROBLEM

Stop Spraying and Praying

Most people approach job searching reactively, without a strategy, relying on volume over targeting. The result is exhaustion, low response rates, and compromised confidence.

Applying the KSS Diagnostic Method: A job search is a strategic campaign. If your resume isn't converting, we diagnose the narrative. If you're missing out on interviews, we diagnose the targeting. We apply the same root-cause analysis to your career trajectory as we do to our business consulting clients.
